What's the issue?

In a gaming world dominated by major studios, there's not enough emphasis for interactive media that can actually tackle critical social issues.

There's a distinct need for creative and thought-provoking platformer games as gaming often prioritizes entertainment over meaning. This creates a clear demand for innovative platformer games that are both creative and thought-provoking, paving the way for a new era of meaningful gaming experiences.

The Brief

Create a concept for a 2D platform game and create the first game level as a prototype. The level has to take at least 3 minutes to play and must involve various obstacles for your character to overcome.

Who is our Intended Audience?

Nova is made for gaming enthusiasts who have a passion for indie gaming.

These enthusiasts typically engage through online and offline gaming communities that act as hubs for discussions, information sharing, and collaboration. They are fertile ground for starting a conversation about and spreading the idea of creative and thought-provoking platformer games with social significance, as members are typically open to exploring innovative approaches within the gaming world and can help promote and support such initiatives.

The Solution

While traditional video games often neglect the potential for deep, empathetic storytelling in favor of more conventional gaming experiences, Nova aims to challenge this trend by offering players a unique opportunity to navigate the delicate space of a young girl's subconscious mind.

My game is inspired by typical child anxieties. Our hero is a young girl, Nova, who ventures through her dreams each night- overcoming her fears and anxieties by completing each game level and destroying the enemy monsters.

Game Environment

The Hero Character

Abilities:Shoot magic fireballs

Weakness: Nightmare Monster enemies that represent her waking anxieties

Character Development

The Enemies

Each one of the enemies represent an anxiety experienced when coming of age. These fears and anxities manifest in our dreams.


Judgement

This enemy is Judgement. Children may be anxious about social situations and fear rejection, ridicule, or being judged by others.


Fear of Death

Children may be afraid of dying, or the people they care about dying, which can be caused by religious or cultural beliefs, past experiences with death or a lack of understanding about the nature of death


Trouble with understanding emotions

As children grow and develop, they experience new feelings and emotions as a reaction to the world around them. They may struggle to articulate and express these emotions they experience as they themselves may not fully be able to understand and navigate their emotions which can lead to a sense of not having control.
