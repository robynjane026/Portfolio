SAY HELLO

Say hello to your new friends who are totally
not going to take over the world.

What's the issue?

It was always thought that in the world of design, our jobs are irreplaceable. However, recent AI development in the design industry, us creatives are in an uncomfortable space.

Could AI really replace our jobs? Or can we work in collaboration with Ai? As we navigate this new space, the challenge is not to simply adapt but to define our future roles in a design world being reshaped by artificial intelligence.

The Brief

Design a prototype of a hyper realistic 3d product and user interface that alleviates this problem. The design should appeal to the needs of the parent as well as the needs of the child.
Who is Our Intended Audience?
The intended audience for this website would be creatives who enjoy engaging in the conversations surrounding the future of AI in the workplace. It includes a a diverse group of individuals with a strong interest in the intersection of creativity and artificial intelligence, specifically in the context of the workplace.

This audience values thoughtful discussions and debates on the implications of AI. They actively seek to stay informed about the latest developments in AI technology and its consequences in the creative workforce.

The Solution

This experimental website starts a conversation around the idea that AI has the potential to be used for both good and bad and how it's up to us as humans to choose how we respond to and manage it.

This website aims to encourage users to contemplate the ethical and practical implications of AI and realize that humans play a vital role in shaping the future of AI and it's dynamic relationship with humanity.
