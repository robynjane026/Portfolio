What's the issue?

In South Africa, a child goes missing every five hours, according to the stats from the South African Police Service Missing Persons Bureau. And, according to the data from Missing Children South Africa, 23% of these kids are never found.

In a world where parents are constantly worried for their child's safety and kids are always looking for adventure, there's a real need to strike a balance all without having the pressure of their parents' anxiety looming over them. As children grow so does their need for independence.

The Brief

Design a prototype of a hyper realistic 3d product and user interface that alleviates this problem. The design should appeal to the needs of the parent as well as the needs of the child.

Who is Our Intended Audience?

Our product is designed for parents who want to ensure their children's safety while encouraging their independence. Whether you're a concerned parent who wants peace of mind, a tech-savvy parent embracing smart solutions, or a working parent juggling responsibilities, our location tracker provides a reliable way to stay connected with your child, no matter where they are.

Adventure-seeking kids and independent teenagers find a friend in our product. It's ideal for youngsters who love exploring and older kids striving for autonomy while giving their parents the reassurance of staying connected in real time. Our tracker adapts to your child's evolving needs as they grow and explore the world.

The Solution

Considering that parents do not realize that their children are missing or may be in danger before it is too late to locate them, a location tracker device designed for adventurous children in mind will aid in alleviating this problem.

This product is unique in a way that it is a lifelong product - it meets the user's need of a parent and a child and adapts to meet those needs as the child grows older and more independant.

Our Product has 3 Sides to it

Our product is a lifelong product made with a child's evolving needs in mind. Phase 1 is intended for younger kids whose parents would be monitoring their location at all times through the Beacon App. As the child grows older so does their need for independence. They have the option to open up the toy to reveal our Phase 2 smart necklace which allows location tracking only when permitted by the young device user.
