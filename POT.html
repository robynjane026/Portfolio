Why 'Short Film?'

Independent film companies and student films enrich the film industry by fostering creativity, diversity, and innovation, creating a more vibrant and inclusive cinematic landscape. They play a pivotal role in the film industry as they bring fresh, diverse perspectives that are unburdened by commercial constraints and offer a broader range of stories and voices.

These filmmakers are unafraid to experiment with innovative storytelling techniques, pushing boundaries and potentially revolutionizing the art of filmmaking as they often spotlight cultures, communities, and narratives that are underrepresented in mainstream cinema, fostering a deeper understanding of human diversity.

Independent filmmakers often share authentic and personal narratives free from commercial pressures, and in this way, often fill gaps in the market by exploring niche genres and themes that may be overlooked by major studios.

The Brief

Create an original 10-20 minute short film on your subject of choice in collaboration with your team. Carry out your roles and responsibilities for the unique role assigned to you.

Who is our Intended Audience?

The intended audience would be Psychological Thriller Enthusiasts who are deeply intrigued by thought-provoking narratives that delve into the human psyche and challenge conventional perceptions of reality. They enjoy themes of ambiguity, unsettling atmospheres, and storytelling that breaks from the norm.

"Pretty Odd Things" is tailored to captivate and engage these our intended audience with it's themes of disillusionment, intrusive thoughts, and exploration of the boundaries between reality and fiction.

The Solution

While mainstream films heavily rely on the potential to prioritize commercial considerations and profit-driven motives over artistic and diverse storytelling, independent film companies and student films enrich the film industry by fostering creativity, diversity, and innovation, creating a more vibrant and inclusive cinematic landscape. They bring fresh, diverse perspectives that are unburdened by commercial constraints and offer a broader range of stories and voices.

Our film aims to explore themes of reality vs fiction, disillusionment, and intrusive thoughts. Through compelling storytelling and evocative visuals, "Pretty Odd Things" will engage viewers in a strange, thrilling and syncopated experience and leave them questioning the boundaries between reality and the mind's experience of reality.


The Story

Emma's troubled relationship with her parents drives her to rather ‘odd’ coping mechanisms involving a reliance on pebbles to make day-to-day decisions. The psychological thriller, 'Pretty Odd Things,' explores the blurring of reality and strange occurrences, revealing the anger of girlhood amidst shadows, stones, and bones.
