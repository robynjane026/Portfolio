What's the issue?

When COVID-19 hit, South African comedians started live-streaming their shows, bringing not only top-notch entertainment but also a feeling of togetherness and unity to their fans.

With the pandemic coming to an end, a new way of entertainment has emerged. The problem? South African comedians are scattered all over different streaming platforms, making it challenging for viewers who just want to kick back and have a good laugh.

The Brief

Establish a single, user-friendly platform where audiences can easily discover and enjoy local South African comedy without the hassle of jumping between multiple services and platforms. Then create a motion graphic identity campaign for a streaming platform that alleviates this problem.

The campaigns message, look and feel and application should be coherent on all touch points, communicating the brandâ€™s values, approach, and unique voice.

Who is Our Intended Audience?

Jislaaik is designed for South Africans who appreciate and want to engage with South African culture and humor.

Our viewers are not only drawn to use this platform for entertainment but are also drawn to immersing themselves in the cultural richness of South Africa through South African comedy.

The Solution

A proudly South African video streaming subscription service for all South African born and bred comedy. Featuring comedians such as Schalk Bezuidenhout, Trevor Noah, and more.

The streaming platform will provide a unified platform that provides context, insights, and a deeper cultural connection alongside comedy content, serving as a gateway to explore and celebrate South African comedy.

This will create a space that promotes the comedic industry and allows viewers find the content they love all in one place.
